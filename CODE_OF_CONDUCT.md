# Code of Conduct

AI Security Lab is intended to be a professional, practitioner-focused community.

Participants are expected to:

- treat others with respect
- critique ideas without attacking people
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and intimidation
- respect confidentiality and disclosure boundaries
- avoid unsolicited sales activity
- disclose relevant commercial interests
- make space for differing levels of experience

Maintainers may remove content or participation that undermines these expectations.

To report a concern, contact the AI Security Lab organisers through the official TechRiot communication channels.
